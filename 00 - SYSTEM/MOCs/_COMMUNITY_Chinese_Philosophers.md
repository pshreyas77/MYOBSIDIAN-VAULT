## Core Concepts

### Confucianism
- Ren (仁) - Humaneness
- Li (禮) - Ritual propriety
- Xiao (孝) - Filial piety
- Junzi - Exemplary person

### Daoism
- Dao (道) - The Way
- Wu Wei - Non-action
- Ziran - Naturalness
- Yin-Yang balance
