## Temple Comparison Research - 2026-04-14

**Source:** Multi-tradition architectural analysis combining Sumerian, Egyptian, Indian, and Chinese data

### Key Findings Summary

**AGE RANKING (Oldest First):**
1. Göbekli Tepe (Turkey) - 9600 BCE
2. Eridu Temple (Sumer) - 5000 BCE  
3. Ggantija (Malta) - 3600 BCE
4. Karnak begins (Egypt) - ~2000 BCE
5. Confucian Temple Qufu (China) - 478 BCE
6. Sanchi Stupa (India, Buddhist) - ~3rd BCE
7. Hindu stone temples (India) - ~4th CE

**BUDDHIST vs HINDU TEMPLES (India):**
- Buddhist stupas (Sanchi, Bodh Gaya, Ajanta) predate Hindu stone temples by 600-700 years
- Buddhist architecture older, more globally distributed
- Hindu temples = larger average complex size, more elaborate per temple

**CHINA CHANGES EVERYTHING:**
- Pre-Cultural Revolution: ~400,000 temples destroyed (worst religious destruction event)
- Current count: ~17,600 Buddhist + ~9,000 Taoist + ~1,700 Confucian + millions of folk shrines
- Oldest Chinese temple: White Horse Temple, Luoyang (~68 CE)
- Oldest Confucian: Qufu (~478 BCE) - predates Indian stone temples by 900 years

**GLOBAL RANKING (Total Sites):**
1. Chinese traditions - tens of millions
2. Hinduism - ~2 million
3. Buddhism (non-China) - ~500,000+
4. Christianity - ~500,000 churches

**MOST SIGNIFICANT:**
- Buddhist structures predate Hindu temples on Indian soil by 700 years
- Cultural Revolution 1966-1976 = worst single event for religious heritage destruction
- IVC (Indus Valley Civilization) = only Bronze Age civilization with NO temples

**Largest Complexes:**
- Angkor Wat (Hindu→Buddhist): 400 acres
- Karnak (Egypt): 200 acres  
- Forbidden City (China): 180 acres
- Ranganathaswamy (Hindu): 156 acres

**Irony:** Buddhism largely expelled from India by ~12th CE, yet its architecture predates Hindu temples on Indian soil.
