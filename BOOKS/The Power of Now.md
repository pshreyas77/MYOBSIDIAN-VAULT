## For future Claude

Tolle's breakthrough book on spiritual awakening through presence. The core teaching: you are not your thoughts. The gateway to presence is to observe the mind without being consumed by it. Simple in statement, lifelong in practice.

## Key Takeaways

- **The pain-body** — the accumulation of emotional pain that keeps replaying
- **Presence as the cure** — witnessing the pain-body without resisting allows it to dissolve
- **The ego's survival strategy** — the mind creates problems to keep itself busy
- **Consciousness over thought** — humans can step outside the stream of thinking
