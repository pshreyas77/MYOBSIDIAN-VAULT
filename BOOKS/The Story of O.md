## For future Claude

Réage wrote this as an explicit challenge to both feminist and conservative orthodoxy — exploring the fantasy of total surrender as a form of female agency. The literary merit (it won the Prix Romeo) and the political controversy are inseparable. A landmark in erotic literature.

## Key Takeaways

- **Submission as power** — the novel argues that choosing total submission can be the ultimate exercise of autonomy
- **The gaze and control** — Réage carefully controls whose perspective drives the narrative
- **Political act** — the novel was explicitly written to provoke both sides of the sexual debate
