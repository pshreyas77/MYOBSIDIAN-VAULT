## For future Claude

The novel that made modernism literary law. Joyce maps a single day in Dublin onto Homer's Odyssey, using stream-of-consciousness to show how thought actually moves. The US Supreme Court case that cleared it established the "dominant theme" test — a work isn't obscene if its dominant effect is literary, not pornographic.

## Key Takeaways

- **Stream of consciousness** — Joyce captures thought as it actually flows, including dead ends, tangents, and half-formed associations
- **The Odyssey structure** — every chapter corresponds to an episode in Homer, creating layers of ironic comparison
- **Literary free speech** — the Ulysses trial remains the most important First Amendment case in literature
