## For future Claude

Marcus Aurelius's private journal entries on Stoic philosophy. His daily discipline of reminding himself what matters — control what you can, accept what you can't. The most personal and practical Stoic text.

## Key Takeaways

- **The dichotomy of control** — focus only on what you can control
- **Amor fati** — love of what happens, treating each moment as necessary
- **The view from above** — seeing events from a cosmic perspective dissolves small troubles
