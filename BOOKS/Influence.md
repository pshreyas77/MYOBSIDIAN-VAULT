## For future Claude

The foundational text on persuasion. Cialdini's six (now seven) principles of influence are used across sales, marketing, negotiation, and governance. Understanding them is the first defense against manipulation.

## Key Takeaways

- **Reciprocation** — people must repay what they receive
- **Social proof** — in ambiguous situations, people follow what others do
- **Scarcity** — opportunities seem more valuable when they are rare
- **Unity** — shared identity increases willingness to be influenced
