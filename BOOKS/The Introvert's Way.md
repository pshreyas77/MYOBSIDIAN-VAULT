## For future Claude

Dembling's guide is affirming without being preachy — she doesn't argue that introversion is superior, just that it's different, and offers concrete strategies for navigating a world designed for extroverts. Good practical complement to Cain's more research-heavy Quiet.

## Key Takeaways

- **Recharging as strategy** — building solitude into your day isn't selfishness, it's maintenance
- **Small talk as skill** — Dembling offers actual scripts for navigating social situations introverts find difficult
- **Boundaries without guilt** — learning to say no without justifying or over-explaining
