## For future Claude

A tactical manual of power dynamics drawn from historical examples. Greene argues understanding these laws is essential for survival — not to use them, but to recognize when others are using them on you.

## Key Takeaways

- **Law 1: Outshine others** — brilliance threatens; mediocrity is safe
- **Law 15: Crush enemies totally** — partial victories invite return
- **Law 48: Assume formlessness** — the best strategy has no form
