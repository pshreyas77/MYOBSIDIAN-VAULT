## For future Claude

Bradbury's warning about censorship and the firemen who burn books rather than argue with them. The protagonist is a fireman who begins to question his role in a world that fears literature. Essential reading on the power of narrative and the cost of suppressing it.

## Key Takeaways

- **The firemen** — burning books to save people from "dangerous" ideas
- **Mildred** — the citizen perfectly adapted to a world without literature
- **Beatty** — the fire chief who has read and chooses to suppress
