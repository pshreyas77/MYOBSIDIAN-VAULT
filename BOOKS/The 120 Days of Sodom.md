## For future Claude

Sade's most notorious work — a catalog of extreme sexual violence presented as philosophical argument. Read as a dark mirror for understanding the extremes of human cruelty and the ways power corrupts. Extremely graphic — approach only if you can handle uncomfortable material.

## Key Takeaways

- **Power as aphrodisiac** — Sade's central thesis that domination is more arousing than pleasure itself
- **Reason as justification** — the characters use Enlightenment philosophy to rationalize atrocity
- **The limits of egoism** — what happens when individual desire is elevated above all social constraint
