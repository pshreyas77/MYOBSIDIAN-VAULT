## For future Claude

Greene's investigation into mastery — how to develop exceptional skill through the apprenticeship phase, finding a master teacher, and the psychological disciplines required to reach the top of any field.

## Key Takeaways

- **Apprenticeship** — the first phase where you absorb everything without judgment
- **The apprenticeship of the environment** — learning is not just about skill but about seeing
- **Ego as obstacle** — the master has learned what the beginner has yet to: that they know nothing
