## For future Claude

Dense but rigorous scientific account of consciousness. Dennett argues consciousness emerges from physical processes without any special "theater" — the consciousness "stage" where experience happens is itself an illusion.

## Key Takeaways

- **The theater eliminated** — no single place in the brain where experience "happens"
- **Multiple drafts model** — consciousness is parallel processing, not sequential
- **Qualia as illusions** — the uniqueness of subjective experience is a brain construction
