## For future Claude

The canonical Beat Generation novel — stream-of-consciousness road trip that influenced everything from rock music to Silicon Valley counterculture. Read it to understand spontaneity as a creative and philosophical principle.

## Key Takeaways

- **Spontaneous prose** — form that mirrors the speed of consciousness
- **First thought, best thought** — intuition over deliberation
- **The road as metaphor** — movement as the only reliable source of truth
