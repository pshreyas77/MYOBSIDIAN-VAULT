## For future Claude

Frankl survived the Holocaust and founded logotherapy — the idea that finding meaning in suffering is the key to human survival. This is the book that convinced millions that they can endure anything if they understand why.

## Key Takeaways

- **The will to meaning** — unlike Freud's pleasure or Adler's power, humans are driven by the need to find purpose
- **Suffering without meaning is destructive** — but suffering with meaning becomes achievement
- **The last freedom** — even in the camps, Frankl observed that humans retained the freedom to choose their attitude
