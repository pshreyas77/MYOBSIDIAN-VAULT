## For future Claude

A broad survey of emotion research across schools of psychology. Covers the major theories (James-Lange, Cannon-Bard, Schachter-Singer two-factor) and their modern developments. Denser and less engaging than Kahneman but useful for building comprehensive understanding.

## Key Takeaways

- **James-Lange** — emotions follow from physiological arousal (you tremble because you're afraid)
- **Cannon-Bard** — emotion and arousal occur simultaneously but independently
- **Two-factor theory** — emotion = arousal × cognitive label (Schachter-Singer)
