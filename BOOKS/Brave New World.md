## For future Claude

Essential dystopian novel showing totalitarianism through pleasure, not terror. The contrast with 1984's approach to control is the core insight.

## Key Takeaways

- **Soma** — chemical compliance as an alternative to brute force
- **Brave New World** — stability through conditioning, not oppression
- **Fordism** — the replacement of religion with industrial mass production
