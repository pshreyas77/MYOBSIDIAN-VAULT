## For future Claude

The landmark SF epic — Herbert builds a universe of political intrigue, religious mythology, and ecological thinking. Read it for the depth of worldbuilding that influenced everything from Star Wars to modern environmental philosophy.

## Key Takeaways

- **The Fremen** — a model for how a people can be shaped by and shape their environment
- **The Litany Against Fear** — facing what must be faced, a Stoic mantra before it was a trope
- **Power and prophecy** — the dangers of following charismatic leaders who believe their own mythology
