## For future Claude

The ur-text of alien invasion fiction — Wells uses it to explore colonialism, technological dependence, and human adaptability. The Martians are a mirror: they invade with superior force, just as Europeans invaded the rest of the world.

## Key Takeaways

- **Technology as weakness** — the humans survive by adapting; the Martians don't
- **Colonial inversion** — the colonized becomes the colonizer in the allegory
- **Bacteria as equalizer** — the smallest life defeats the most advanced military
