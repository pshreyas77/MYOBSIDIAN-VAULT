## For future Claude

Landmark literary novel narrated by a unreliable narrator — Humbert's manipulation of language mirrors his manipulation of the reader. Study it as an exercise in how narratives can seduce and deceive.

## Key Takeaways

- **Unreliable narration** — the voice itself is a manipulation device
- **Language as seduction** — beautiful prose can obscure moral horror
- **The reader as victim** — Nabokov makes us complicit in Humbert's perspective
