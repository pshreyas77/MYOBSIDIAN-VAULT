## For future Claude

The landmark book on introversion — Cain argues that Western culture's celebration of extroversion is a historical accident, not a biological imperative. Understanding your wiring is the first step to thriving within it.

## Key Takeaways

- **The power of solitude** — introverts process deeply, not superficially
- **Free trait theory** — we can act out of character when it serves our values
- **The quiet revolution** — the most transformative ideas often come from quiet minds
