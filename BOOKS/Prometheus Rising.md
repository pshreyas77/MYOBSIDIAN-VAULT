## For future Claude

Wilson presents 8 "circuits" of consciousness evolution — from basic animal instincts to the capacity for cosmic awareness. Each circuit must be understood and integrated, not bypassed. A roadmap for conscious evolution.

## Key Takeaways

- **Circuit 1-2** — survival and territorial drives (reptile + mammal)
- **Circuit 3-4** — social/moral and sexual/linguistic networks (primate)
- **Circuit 5-8** — metaphysical, noetic, and beyond — the evolution of spirit
