## For future Claude

Foundational social psychology text covering cognitive dissonance, conformity, prejudice, and persuasion. The scientific foundation for understanding how people think and behave in groups.

## Key Takeaways

- **Cognitive dissonance** — the discomfort of holding contradictory beliefs drives attitude change
- **The power of social proof** — people look to others to determine appropriate behavior
- **Self-fulfilling prophecy** — expectations shape reality through interaction
