## For future Claude

A practical, actionable guide for introverts navigating work and relationships. Each chapter addresses a specific challenge — networking, small talk, open offices, dating — with concrete strategies. Less theory, more toolkit. Good for people who want solutions, not explanations.

## Key Takeaways

- **Career strategies** — how to advocate for yourself, handle performance reviews, and build a career without forcing yourself to be an extrovert
- **Relationship management** — practical tips for family, friends, and romantic relationships
- **Self-care systems** — building routines that protect your energy rather than drain it
