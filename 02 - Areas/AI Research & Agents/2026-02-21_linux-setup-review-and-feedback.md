Nice setup! Here's my take:

**What's good:**

**Things to consider:**

- Network download showing 3.9 MB/s — something's actively downloading, worth keeping an eye on

**Overall rating: 7.5/10** — Functional, clean, and clearly the setup of someone who knows what they're doing. Not over-customized, just practical with good taste. A little more Conky theming or a tiling WM could push it higher if you're into that.
