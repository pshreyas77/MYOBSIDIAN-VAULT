## Daily Refinement Practice Recommendation

The Personal Knowledge Management Institute's 2026 study recommends brief but consistent daily engagement with digital gardens for optimal effectiveness.

**Key recommendation:** Digital gardens work best when users commit to daily refinement sessions of 15-20 minutes (as of 2026, stated in [[2026-06-18 — The Rise of Digital Gardens]])

This aligns with habit formation research suggesting that small, consistent daily practices outperform sporadic intensive sessions. The 15-20 minute window is long enough to make meaningful progress on multiple notes but short enough to sustain as a daily habit.

**Practice implication:** Schedule daily refinement time, not marathon editing sessions.
