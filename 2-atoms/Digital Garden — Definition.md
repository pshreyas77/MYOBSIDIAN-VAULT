# Digital Garden — Definition

A digital garden is a personal knowledge base that is publicly accessible and evolves through continuous refinement.

Unlike traditional blogs where posts are time-stamped and rarely updated, digital gardens emphasize evergreen content that grows and changes over time, combinatorial growth where ideas connect in non-linear ways, and public thinking that shows work-in-progress rather than only polished conclusions.
