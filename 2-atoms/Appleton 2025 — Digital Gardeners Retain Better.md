## The Finding

Maggie Appleton's 2025 research found that **67% of digital gardeners report improved retention of complex concepts** compared to linear note-taking.

This suggests that the act of continuously refining and connecting notes — rather than writing once and publishing — strengthens memory and understanding of the material.
