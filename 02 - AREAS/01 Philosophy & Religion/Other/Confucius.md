# Confucius (Kong Qiu)

**Born**: ~551 BCE, Lu state (China)  
**Died**: ~479 BCE  
**Text**: Analects ( recorded by disciples)  
**Influence**: Chinese state philosophy ~200 BCE–1911 CE (2,100 years)  
**Status**: #entity #philosopher

---

## Philosophy

### Ren (仁) — Humaneness/Benevolence
- Central virtue
- Treat others as you would be treated (Golden Rule)

### Li (禮) — Ritual Propriety
- Proper conduct in every relationship
- Social harmony through correct behavior

### Five Relationships
1. Ruler-Subject
2. Father-Son
3. Husband-Wife
4. Elder-Younger
5. Friend-Friend (only equal one)

### Junzi (君子) — Noble Person/Gentleman
- Moral ideal
- Cultivated through education and ritual

## Comparison: Confucius vs Buddha

| Criterion | Confucius | Buddha |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Metaphysics | Agnostic | Avyakata (refused speculation) |
| Social ethics | Hierarchical | Egalitarian (rejected caste) |
| Suffering | Social disorder | Craving/attachment |
| Solution | Moral cultivation | Eightfold Path |
| Government | Virtuous ruler | Dhamma-based (Ashoka) |

**Parallel**: Both refused metaphysical speculation. Both focused on practical ethics. Both transformed civilizations.

**Difference**: Confucius accepted hierarchy. Buddha rejected it.

---

## Comparison: Confucius vs Chanakya

| Criterion | Confucius | Chanakya |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| Ideal | Moral junzi advising virtue | Strategic operator serving state |
| Human nature | Improvable, learnable | Self-interested, must be managed |
| Means | Ritual, education | Power, intelligence, fear |
| Legacy | 2100 years philosophy | Arthashastra studied globally |

**Verdict**: Confucius = idealist. Chanakya = realist. History validated both.

**Practical validation**: Chanakya built working empire. Confucius wandered 14 years finding no ruler willing to implement his ideas.

## Impact

Most historically impactful philosopher: 2,100 years as Chinese state philosophy.

Shaped: China, Korea, Japan, Vietnam (~1.5 billion people today).
