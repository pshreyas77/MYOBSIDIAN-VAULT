Knowledge Destruction · 1193 CE
Nalanda University Ruins
Bihar, India · UNESCO World Heritage Site
Greatest Buddhist university. Library burned for months by Bakhtiyar Khilji in 1193 CE. Vast Buddhist texts in philosophy, medicine and science lost. The last major institutional centre of Buddhism in India.

Final Scholarly Verdict — What Is Confirmed vs. What Is Not
✓ Buddhist structures predate Hindu temples by 658 yearsLumbini 550 BCE vs Mundeshwari 108 CE — archaeologically confirmed.
✓ Buddha not mentioned in any VedaComplete silence across all four Vedas, Upanishads, and the Bhagavad Gita.
✓ Avatar theory appears 900+ years after Buddha diedVishnu Purana ~400–500 CE. Called "Mayamoha" — a divine deception, not a celebration.
✓ Buddhism spread globally before HinduismAshoka's mission: Egypt, Afghanistan, Sri Lanka — all 3rd century BCE.
✗ "Buddhism came before ALL Hindu traditions"The Rigveda (~1500 BCE) predates Buddhism. The Vedic tradition is older.
✗ "All Hindu temples built on Buddhist sites"Some documented cases exist. The Shungas actually preserved Sanchi.
