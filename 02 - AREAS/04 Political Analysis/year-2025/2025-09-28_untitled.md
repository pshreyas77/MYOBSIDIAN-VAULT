## 🎮 **What's Implemented:**

**Core Souls-like Features:**
- **Stamina-based combat** - Light attacks (J) and heavy slide tackles (K)
- **Corner flag checkpoints** - Rest with R to restore health/stamina and respawn enemies
- **FIFA Points currency** - Collect from defeated enemies to level up
- **Death mechanics** - "YOU DIED" screen with respawn at corner flag
- **Invincibility frames** after taking damage

**Football-themed Combat:**
- **Ball mechanics** - Kick with SPACE, physics-based movement
- **Enemy types** matching our concept:
  - **Winger Wraiths** (red, fast flankers)
  - **Sweeper Golems** (brown, tanky guards) 
  - **Referee Revenants** (black, cast stamina-draining "red cards")

**Progression System:**
- Level up by spending FIFA Points at corner flags
- Increased health and stamina per level
- Visual feedback with glowing effects and health bars

## 🕹️ **Controls:**
- **WASD** - Move around the pitch
- **SHIFT** - Sprint (drains stamina)
- **SPACE** - Kick ball when nearby
- **J** - Light attack (quick, low stamina)
- **K** - Heavy attack (slide tackle, high damage/stamina)
- **R** - Rest at corner flag (when close)

The atmosphere captures that dark, cursed stadium feeling with the gothic color scheme and glowing effects. Try different combat strategies - the ball can be used tactically, and positioning matters for the different enemy types!
