# My Political Stance

I support Periyar's atheism and Ambedkar's constitution rights, social justice, and equality.

This is based on discussions with the caveman skill about Indian politics, where I expressed that I follow Type 1 and 2 secularism, not Type 3 (pseudo-secularism).

I believe in the Ambedkar+Periyar model for anti-caste and secular politics.
