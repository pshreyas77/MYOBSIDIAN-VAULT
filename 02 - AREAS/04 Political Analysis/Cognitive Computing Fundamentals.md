## **1. Cognitive Computing as a New Generation**

### 📘 **Simple Explanation:**
Cognitive computing is the **next evolution of AI** — it doesn’t just follow rules, but **learns, reasons, and adapts like a human brain**.

### 🔹 **Key Idea:**
- Traditional computing = **automation**  
- Cognitive computing = **augmentation** of human intelligence

### 📌 **Diagram:**

```
Traditional Computing → Rule-based, fixed logic
          ↓
Cognitive Computing → Learns, adapts, reasons with uncertainty
```

## **1. Preparing for Change**

### 📘 **Simple Explanation:**
Businesses must **adapt** to cognitive computing or risk falling behind. It’s a shift from **rule-based automation** to **intelligent, learning systems**.

### 🔹 **Key Actions:**
- Train employees  
- Update IT infrastructure  
- Change business processes  
- Foster innovation culture

### 📌 **Diagram:**

```
Traditional Business → Fixed Processes
         ↓
Cognitive Business → Learning, Adapting, Evolving
```
