Ah, The Living Tribunal! This is a fascinating matchup between two of the most powerful cosmic entities in their respective universes.

**Michael Demiurgos (DC/Vertigo):**
- One of two Demiurgic powers alongside Lucifer Morningstar
- Possesses the power of creation itself (while Lucifer has the power of will/shaping)
- Can create entire universes and realities
- Exists beyond most conventional concepts of space and time
- Has remade portions of the DC multiverse
- Considered second only to The Presence (DC's version of God)

**The Living Tribunal (Marvel):**
- The multiversal judge and arbiter of cosmic balance
- Authority over the entire Marvel multiverse
- Can erase universes or entire timelines from existence
- Has three faces representing equity, necessity, and vengeance
- Nearly omnipotent, answering only to The One-Above-All

**The Matchup:**
This would be incredibly close. Both operate at multiversal+ levels of power. The Living Tribunal has more consistent feats of multiversal authority and judgment, while Michael has the raw creative force that shaped DC's cosmology.

Most debates lean slightly toward The Living Tribunal due to his more concrete multiversal feats and authority, but it would be an extremely close battle between two near-omnipotent beings.
