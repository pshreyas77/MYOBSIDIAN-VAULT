## 🔍 What You're Describing is Called **OSINT**
Open Source Intelligence — the practice of gathering information from publicly available sources while protecting your identity.

## 🛡️ STEP 1: Protect Your Identity First

### Stay Anonymous Online
| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| **Tor Browser** | Routes traffic through multiple nodes, hides IP | Free |
| **Tails OS** | Live OS that leaves no trace on your computer | Free |
| **Whonix** | VM-based anonymous OS | Free |
| **ProtonVPN** | Encrypted VPN (free tier available) | Free/Paid |
| **Mullvad VPN** | No-logs VPN, accepts cash/crypto | ~$5/mo |

### Anonymous Accounts
- Use **ProtonMail** or **Tutanota** for anonymous email
- Create accounts over Tor, never from your real IP
- Use **SimpleLogin** for throwaway email aliases
- Never reuse usernames across platforms

## 🕵️ STEP 3: Find Hidden & Deep Sources

### The "Invisible Web"
Most of the internet isn't indexed by Google. Access it with:

- **Google Dorks** — advanced search operators to find hidden files:
  - `filetype:pdf site:gov "confidential"`
  - `inurl:admin intitle:login`
  - `"index of" /passwords`
- **Ahmia.fi** — search engine for Tor hidden services (legal content)
- **OSINT Framework** (osintframework.com) — giant map of every research tool by category

### Leaked & Archived Data
- **Archive.org** — archived websites going back decades
- **Wikileaks** — declassified/leaked government documents
- **DocumentCloud** — investigative journalists' document archive
- **GovInfo.gov** — US government documents, reports, hearings
- **PACER** (public court records, US)
- **OpenCorporates** — world's largest open database of companies

### Academic & Scientific Hidden Research
- **Sci-Hub** — access to paywalled research papers
- **Unpaywall** — legal free version of paywalled papers
- **BASE** (base-search.net) — 300M+ academic documents
- **CORE** — aggregates open-access research

### Financial & Corporate Records
- **OpenSecrets** — US political money flows
- **ICIJ Offshore Leaks** — Panama/Pandora Papers database
- **EDGAR (SEC)** — US company financial filings
- **Companies House (UK)** — UK company records free
- **FollowTheMoney.org** — tracks money in US politics

## 💻 STEP 4: Set Up a Safe Research Environment

### Recommended Setup (Free)
```
1. Download Tails OS → boot from USB (leaves zero trace)
2. Connect to Tor or VPN inside Tails
3. Use Tor Browser for all research browsing
4. Keep research notes in VeraCrypt encrypted containers
5. Never do personal things (email, banking) while researching
```

### Operational Security (OpSec) Rules
- 🚫 Never research from home WiFi without VPN+Tor
- 🚫 Never log into personal accounts during research sessions
- 🚫 Never take screenshots with personal devices (metadata)
- ✅ Use a dedicated cheap laptop for research only
- ✅ Use public WiFi + Tor for sensitive research
- ✅ Encrypt all notes with **VeraCrypt** (free)
- ✅ Use **KeePassXC** for anonymous account passwords
