**Conclusion:** Ea-Nasir is older than Vedic religion, but not by thousands of years—only ~250–550 years.

## 4. Ancient Greek vs Hinduism (Vedic)

| Tradition | Confirmed Start | Evidence |
|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| **Mycenaean Greek** | ~1600 BCE | Linear B tablets, palace archaeology **confirmed** |
| **Vedic religion** | ~1500–1200 BCE | Rigveda, fire altars **confirmed** |
| Classical Greek | ~800–700 BCE | Homer, Hesiod, temple archaeology **confirmed** |

**Critical insight:** Vedic AND Greek both descend from **Proto-Indo-European religion ~3500–2500 BCE**:
- Dyaus Pita (Vedic) = Zeus Pater (Greek) = same word, same root
- Indra ↔ Zeus (sky-father)
- Neither "invented" religion — both inherited PIE framework
- **Mycenaean Greek (~1600 BCE) is slightly older than Vedic (~1500 BCE) by ~100 years**

[!contradiction] Claim: "Rigveda 4,000 BCE via astronomy dating" — **Contested/rejected by mainstream.**
[!contradiction] Claim: "Hindu temples predate Greek temples" — **FALSE.** Oldest Hindu stone temples ~5th century CE. Greek temples ~700 BCE. Greeks win by 1,000+ years.

## 7. Caste System Origins — Evidence-Based

### Who Brought It

**Steppe migrants + Vedic Brahmanical priests** — not a single import but developed gradually.

### Timeline

| Stage | Period | Development |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| Proto-Varna | ~2000–1500 BCE | Steppe arrival; Arya vs. Dasa divisions |
| Purusha Sukta | ~1200–1000 BCE | **First cosmic justification** for 4 varnas |
| Varna hardens | ~1000–500 BCE | Brahmin monopoly on ritual |
| Jati emerges | ~600–300 BCE | Endogamous sub-castes |
| Legal codification | ~200 BCE–200 CE | **Manusmriti** — full caste system |
| Genetic endogamy locks | ~100 CE | DNA confirms strict boundaries |

### Genetics Confirms

- Steppe arrival ~2000–1500 BCE = confirmed by ancient DNA
- Caste system = genetically confirmed ~2000–1500 BCE
- Indian populations stop genetic mixing ~2000 BCE
- Caste boundaries preserved for **3,000+ years**
- Upper castes = more Steppe ancestry; Lower/Dravidian = more AASI

[!contradiction] Claim: "Caste in IVC" — **FALSE.** IVC egalitarian, no elite class.
[!contradiction] Claim: "Indigenous caste" — System is Vedic Indo-Aryan innovation.
