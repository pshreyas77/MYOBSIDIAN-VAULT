### 🤖 Claude

Wally West would likely win against the Runner in a race or direct confrontation.

Here's why:

**Wally West's advantages:**
- As the Flash, he can tap into the Speed Force, which grants him virtually unlimited speed potential
- Has achieved speeds faster than light, and in some storylines has outrun death itself and travelled through time
- The Speed Force gives him additional abilities beyond just speed - like phasing through matter, creating speed mirages, and stealing kinetic energy
- Has experience fighting cosmic-level threats and other speedsters

**The Runner's abilities:**
- One of the Elders of the Universe in Marvel Comics
- Possesses the Power Primordial, granting him superhuman speed among other cosmic powers
- Has incredible endurance and has been running across the universe for eons
- Definitely faster than light travel capabilities

**The key factor:** While the Runner is incredibly fast on a cosmic scale and has more experience (being billions of years old), Wally West's connection to the Speed Force appears to offer higher speed potential. The Speed Force has been portrayed as a fundamental force of the DC Universe that transcends normal physical limitations.

In most crossover scenarios and fan discussions, characters who can tap into conceptual forces (like the Speed Force) tend to have an edge over those relying purely on physical enhancement, even cosmic-level enhancement.

That said, it would likely be a very close contest, and the Runner's vast experience and cosmic-level durability would make him a formidable opponent even if he couldn't quite match Wally's top speed.
