# India population map by state

> **Conversation Overview**

The person requested Claude to build interactive data visualizations focused on India's population statistics. Using a "godmode" style prompt, they asked for a comprehensive HTML-based interactive map showing India's state-wise population data in millions with present/projected figures. Claude built a full dashboard widget featuring summary stat cards, a D3.js-rendered India map using world-atlas topology, a color-coded legend, a scrollable ranked list of all 36 states and UTs, and a detail panel showing population, national share percentage, rank, and regional comparisons. Population data used was 2026 projections from the MoHFW Technical Group on Population Projections (based on Census 2011), covering all 28 states and 8 UTs.

The person then requested a more detailed state-level choropleth map specifically. The map covered all major states with approximate but geographically representative shapes.

For the third request, the person asked for a direct comparison of all south Indian states' combined population against Uttar Pradesh. Claude built a Chart.js bar chart widget comparing Tamil Nadu (77.6M), Karnataka (69.8M), Andhra Pradesh (52.2M), Telangana (38.7M), and Kerala (36.2M) individually against UP (243.5M), along with a proportional split bar showing south combined vs. UP, KPI cards, and a written verdict summarizing the comparison. The key finding highlighted was that UP alone nearly matches all five southern states combined, and exceeds any individual southern state by a factor of 3x to nearly 7x.
