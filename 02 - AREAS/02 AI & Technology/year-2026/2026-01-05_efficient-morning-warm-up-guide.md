# ⚡ 7–10 MINUTE COMPLETE EARLY-MORNING WARM-UP

**Goal:**
✔ Wake nervous system
✔ Increase core temperature
✔ Lubricate joints
✔ Activate running + workout muscles
✔ Prevent injury when body is stiff & cold

---

## ⏱ TOTAL TIME: **7–10 minutes**

* **Phase 1:** Wake & Heat (2 min)
* **Phase 2:** Joint Lubrication (3 min)
* **Phase 3:** Muscle Activation (2–3 min)
* **Phase 4:** Nervous System Primer (1–2 min)

---

## 🔥 PHASE 1: WAKE + HEAT (2 MIN)

**Do immediately after getting out of bed**

### Do:

* **Jumping Jacks** – 45 sec
* **High Knees (fast)** – 30 sec
* **Arm Swings (big circles)** – 30 sec
* **Light jog in place** – 15 sec

🧠 *Why:* Raises core temperature quickly, switches body from sleep mode to movement mode.

---

## 🔄 PHASE 2: JOINT LUBRICATION (3 MIN)

**No stretching yet — just movement**

![Image](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/5f5e8592d2b0854b18af6975/da514943-4e3d-4d19-af4a-541a194f3781/Quadruped%2BNeck%2BCircles.jpg  )

### Do:

* **Neck circles** – 5 each direction
* **Shoulder rolls** – 10
* **Hip circles** – 10 each direction
* **Ankle circles** – 10 each ankle
* **Torso twists** – 20 sec

🧠 *Why:* Synovial fluid wakes up joints → less stiffness, smoother motion.

### Do:

* **Bodyweight Squats** – 15 reps
* **Forward Lunges** – 5 each leg
* **Glute Bridges** – 10 reps
* **Calf Raises** – 15 reps

🧠 *Why:* Activates glutes, quads, calves, hamstrings → prevents knee & hamstring injuries.

# 🟢 YOU ARE NOW READY TO:

## ❌ WHAT NOT TO DO AT 5 AM

* ❌ Static stretching (touching toes, long holds)
* ❌ Sitting around after waking
* ❌ Cold start sprinting
* ❌ Foam rolling before warm-up

---

## 🧠 OPTIONAL (30-SECOND BOOST)

* Splash **cold water on face**
* Drink **½ glass water**
* 5 deep nasal breaths
