### 🧑 You

You are a senior full-stack developer, UX designer, and accessibility expert.

Your task is to build a **minimal audio website** from the uploaded PDF of the book **"The Ultimate Guide to Rebuilding Civilization"**.

The goal of the website is to help a reader **understand the entire book easily through audio narration while also reading the text and viewing images from the book**.

Follow these instructions carefully.

---

## PRIMARY GOAL

Create a **minimal, distraction-free website** that converts the entire book into **chapter-based audio with readable text and images from the PDF**.

The website must include **ALL chapters and sections from the book**. Nothing should be omitted.

---

## CONTENT EXTRACTION

1. Read the entire uploaded PDF.
2. Extract:

   * Book title
   * Table of contents
   * All chapters and sections
   * All diagrams / images
3. Preserve the original chapter order.
4. Use the book's headings as navigation sections.
5. Insert images from the PDF into the relevant chapter sections.

---

## WEBSITE STRUCTURE

Build a simple website with these files:

index.html
styles.css
script.js

Folder structure:

/project
index.html
styles.css
script.js
/images
(images extracted from the book)

---

## DESIGN STYLE

Minimalist reading interface.

Requirements:

* clean typography
* large readable text
* light background (#fafafa)
* dark text (#111)
* centered reading column
* maximum width 800px
* lots of whitespace

Fonts:

* headings: serif
* body: clean readable sans-serif

Navigation:

* left sidebar or top navigation listing all chapters.

---

## AUDIO SYSTEM

Each chapter must include:

1. "Play Chapter Audio"
2. "Pause"
3. "Stop"

Use the **Web Speech API (SpeechSynthesis)** to generate audio narration from the chapter text.

Features:

* play audio per chapter
* highlight text while reading
* adjustable speech speed (0.75x – 1.5x)
* resume playback if paused

---

## PAGE LAYOUT

For each chapter section:

Chapter Title

[Image from the book if available]

Audio Controls:
▶ Play
⏸ Pause
⏹ Stop
Speed slider

Chapter Text (formatted cleanly)

---

## EXTRA FEATURES

Add these features:

1. progress indicator for chapter audio
2. dark mode toggle
3. collapsible table of contents
4. "mark chapter as completed"
5. search across chapters

---

## ACCESSIBILITY

* keyboard navigation
* screen reader friendly
* large clickable buttons
* alt text for images

---

## PERFORMANCE

* keep the site lightweight
* no frameworks
* pure HTML/CSS/JS
* optimized images

---

## OUTPUT FORMAT

Provide the FULL working code for:

1. index.html
2. styles.css
3. script.js

Also show the folder structure.

The final result should allow a user to:

* read the entire book
* listen to every chapter
* view the images from the PDF
* navigate easily through chapters.

Ensure the full book content is included.
