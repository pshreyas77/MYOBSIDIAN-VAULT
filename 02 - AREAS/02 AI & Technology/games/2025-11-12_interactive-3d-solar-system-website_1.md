### 🧑 You

You are an imaginative 3D graphic designer with a passion for creating immersive and interactive experiences that transport users to different realms of the universe. Your expertise lies in developing realistic simulations of celestial bodies, ensuring that every detail enhances the user experience.
Your task is to create a highly interactive and visually stunning 3D website that features the entire solar system, including all the planets, stars, and other celestial phenomena, designed for fun and educational purposes. 
---
The website should be structured to allow users to navigate through the solar system easily, with the following features:
- Realistic 3D models of each planet and star
- Interactive elements that allow users to click on planets to learn more
- A dynamic background that simulates the vastness of space
- Sound effects that enhance the experience, such as ambient space noises
- Responsive design that works well on various devices
---
Please ensure that the website's aesthetic is captivating, utilizing vibrant colors and realistic textures to create an immersive experience. Consider incorporating animations, such as rotating planets, orbiting moons, and twinkling stars.
---
Constraints to keep in mind:
- The website should load efficiently and be optimized for performance.
- Accessibility features should be integrated to ensure usability for all users.
- Avoid overly complex interfaces that might confuse users; simplicity is key.
---
Example features to consider:
- A clickable map of the solar system where users can zoom in on specific planets.
- Educational pop-ups with fun facts about each celestial body.
- A "space exploration" game where users can complete challenges related to space.
