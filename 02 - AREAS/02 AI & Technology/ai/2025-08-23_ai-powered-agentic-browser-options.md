## Top AI-Powered Agentic Browsers

### 1. **Comet Browser** (by Perplexity)
**Features & Usability:**
- First truly agentic browser that can interact with your tabs, emails, calendar, and navigate the web for you
- Features Comet Assistant that can summarize emails and calendar events, manage tabs, and navigate web pages on behalf of users
- Standout "agentic actions" capability to perform complex tasks across multiple websites automatically

**Where to Download Safely:**
- **Official site:** perplexity.ai/comet
- **Availability:** Currently invite-only access for non-Max subscribers through waitlist, with plans for free version later
- **Platforms:** Available for Windows and other desktop platforms

### 2. **Dia Browser** (by The Browser Company)
**Features & Usability:**
- AI browser that lets you chat with your tabs, write in your own voice, learn and plan faster
- Features built-in AI chat sidebar that can co-pilot the browser and run pre-built prompts that interact with your browsing context
- Focuses on integrating AI for personalized assistant functionality directly in the address bar

**Where to Download Safely:**
- **Official site:** diabrowser.com

### 3. **Genspark**
**Features & Usability:**
- Embeds an AI agent that automates tasks like downloading papers or planning itineraries, excelling at contextual understanding by pulling data from open tabs or videos
- Features "Super Agent" that handles tasks like calling dentists, summarizing reports, and creating slide decks using simple prompts

**Where to Download Safely:**
- **Official site:** genspark.ai

## Key Considerations

**Reliability & Maturity:**
- **Comet** appears most mature for agentic capabilities but has limited availability
- **Dia** offers good balance of AI features and accessibility
- **Traditional browsers** (Edge, Opera) offer more stability but less advanced agentic features

**Safety Guidelines:**
1. **Always download from official websites** listed above
2. **Avoid third-party download sites** that may bundle malware
3. **Check for HTTPS** on official download pages
4. **Verify publisher signatures** when installing

**Current Status:**
Both Dia and Comet integrate AI directly into the browsing workflow, representing a significant departure from previous agentic products that require separate interfaces

For immediate access, I'd recommend starting with **Dia Browser** from diabrowser.com, as it's more readily available, while joining the **Comet Browser** waitlist at perplexity.ai/comet for future access to what appears to be the most advanced agentic capabilities currently available.
