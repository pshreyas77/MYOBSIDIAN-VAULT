### 🤖 Claude

Looking at this matchup, I need to break down each character's peak abilities:

**Aizen (Bleach)**
- Hogyoku fusion grants near-immortality and continuous evolution
- Complete Hypnosis (Kyoka Suigetsu) - absolute sensory manipulation
- Transcendent spiritual pressure that can disintegrate weaker beings
- Immense speed, strength, and reiatsu manipulation

**Makima (Chainsaw Man)**  
- Control Devil powers - can dominate anyone she perceives as inferior
- Contract with Japanese PM - damage transfers to random Japanese citizens
- Can command other devils and humans
- Regeneration and various devil abilities

**Gojo Satoru (Jujutsu Kaisen)**
- Limitless technique creates infinite space manipulation
- Six Eyes provide perfect cursed energy control and perception
- Infinity makes him essentially untouchable
- Domain Expansion: Unlimited Void overwhelms opponents with infinite information

**Analysis:**

Gojo's Infinity is his biggest advantage - it creates an insurmountable barrier that neither Aizen nor Makima have shown the ability to bypass. However, this becomes more complex when considering:

1. **Aizen vs Gojo**: Aizen's Complete Hypnosis could potentially affect Gojo's perception, but Gojo's Six Eyes might provide resistance. If Kyoka Suigetsu works, Aizen wins. If not, Gojo's untouchability makes it a stalemate unless his Domain Expansion can affect a transcendent being.

2. **Makima's Control** might not work on either due to their immense power levels - both likely wouldn't perceive her as superior.

3. **Aizen's immortality** through the Hogyoku means he can potentially outlast both opponents.

**My assessment**: 
- **Tier**: All three are in the "broken hax" tier of their respective verses
- **Winner**: Likely **Aizen**, due to his combination of Complete Hypnosis potentially bypassing defenses, Hogyoku immortality, and transcendent evolution. Gojo's Infinity is nearly unbeatable, but Aizen's sensory manipulation operates on a conceptual level that might circumvent it.

However, this heavily depends on how their abilities interact across different power systems, which is inherently speculative. What's your take on how these hax abilities would interact?
