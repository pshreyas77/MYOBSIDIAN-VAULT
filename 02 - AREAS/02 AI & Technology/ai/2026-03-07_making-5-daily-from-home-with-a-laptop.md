## 🛠️ Step-by-Step Plan

### Step 1 — Pick Your Niche (Day 1)
Choose something simple and searchable:
- Daily motivational quotes for students
- Budget meal ideas for college students
- Simple home workout tips
- Pet care tips for beginners

### Step 2 — Create a Free Blog (Day 1)
1. Go to **blogger.com** (100% free, by Google)
2. Sign in with a Gmail account
3. Click "New Blog" → pick a name like *DailyStudentQuotes.blogspot.com*
4. Choose any template and hit **Create**

### Step 3 — Use AI to Write Content (Day 1–7)
Use **Claude.ai** (this chat!) or **ChatGPT** (free) to generate posts.

**Use this prompt every day to generate a post:**

> *"Write a blog post of 400 words about [your niche topic]. Make it beginner-friendly, add 5 practical tips, and end with a motivational line. Format it with a catchy title and subheadings."*

Publish **1 post per day** for 30 days. Consistency is everything.

### Step 4 — Apply for Google AdSense (After 20–30 posts)
1. Go to **google.com/adsense**
2. Apply with your Blogger blog URL
3. Once approved, Google places ads on your blog automatically
4. You earn money every time someone clicks an ad

**Realistic earnings:**
- 100 visitors/day = ~$0.10–$0.50/day (early stage)
- 1,000 visitors/day = ~$1–$5+/day ✅

### Step 5 — Get Free Traffic (Week 2 onwards)
- Share every post in **Facebook Groups** related to your niche
- Post short versions on **Reddit** (subreddits like r/GetMotivated, r/food, etc.)
- Pin posts on **Pinterest** (huge free traffic source for blogs)

## ✅ Daily Routine (Takes only 30–45 min/day)

1. Open Claude or ChatGPT → generate today's post using the prompt above
2. Copy it to Blogger → add a free image from **Unsplash.com**
3. Share the link in 2–3 Facebook groups or Reddit threads
4. Done ✅

## 🔑 Key Tips for Success

- **Be consistent** — 30 days of daily posting beats everything
- **Stick to one niche** — don't mix topics
- **Use free images** from Unsplash or Pixabay (never copy from Google Images)
- Don't quit before Month 2 — traffic takes time to build
