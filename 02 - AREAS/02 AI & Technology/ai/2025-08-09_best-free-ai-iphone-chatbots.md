## Top 10 Free AI Chatbot Apps for iPhone

1. **ChatGPT** (OpenAI)
   - The most popular AI chatbot with regularly updated iPhone apps
   - Free tier available with GPT-3.5, premium for GPT-4

2. **Microsoft Copilot**
   - Uses OpenAI and Microsoft AI models for conversational chatbot features and image generation, available to use for free
   - Integrates with Microsoft services

3. **Claude** (Anthropic)
   - Available as a free app and web version
   - Known for helpful, harmless, and honest responses

4. **Google Bard/Gemini**
   - Google's AI chatbot with free access
   - Integrated with Google services

5. **Meta AI**
   - Available as a standalone app and built into Facebook and Instagram
   - Free with social media account login

6. **AI Chatbot - Nova**
   - Built on GPT-4, Google Gemini, Claude, and DeepSeek technologies
   - Free version available with premium upgrades

7. **PowerBrain AI Chat**
   - Cutting-edge AI chatbot and virtual assistant designed to provide rapid responses and expert-level insights
   - Free tier with premium features

8. **Chatbox AI**
   - Compatible with many cutting-edge AI models and APIs, available on iOS
   - Multi-platform support

9. **KnowItAll AI Chatbot**
   - Marketed as a free ChatGPT alternative that "gives better answers and can handle far more complex tasks"
   - Free iPhone app

10. **Perplexity AI**
    - AI-powered search and chat combination
    - Free tier with limited queries

Most of these apps offer free versions with basic functionality, though many have premium tiers that unlock additional features like faster responses, more advanced models, or unlimited usage. The free tiers are usually sufficient for casual use and testing different AI personalities and capabilities.
