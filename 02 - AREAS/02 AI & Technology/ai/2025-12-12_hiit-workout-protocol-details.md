### Key Exercises and Performance Cues

#### Lower Body Exercises (HIIT-A "Leg Furnace")

| Exercise | Specific Instruction/Variation from Source |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Jump Squats** | A primary exercise in the 4-round ladder circuit. Can be replaced by **Air Squats** if knees are sensitive. |
| **Split-Squat Jumps** | A plyometric move where you must **switch legs at the 15-second mark** during the 30-second work intervals. |
| **Squat-hold pulses** | Performed as a finisher, holding a **bottom half squat position** and executing **micro-pulses** for 3 minutes. |
| **Jump-lunges** | Used in the Metabolic Circuit for 15 reps per round. |
| **Alternating Reverse Lunge + Knee Drive** | Must be performed **explosively** during the Main Circuit. |
| **Skater hops** | Used in the HIIT-A ladder. |

#### Upper Body and Pushing Exercises

| Exercise | Specific Instruction/Variation from Source |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Push-ups** | Standard push-ups are done for 15–20 reps during the EMOM session. |
| **Decline push-ups** | Performed with your **feet elevated on a sofa** (or sturdy chair). Used for 10 reps in the Metabolic Circuit. |
| **Pike push-ups** | Used during the HIIT-B Tabata session. |
| **Burpee Variations** | Burpees are performed stand-alone or as part of combinations: **Burpee + Push-Up** or **Burpee-to-push-up**. |
| **Squat Thrusts** | Used in the Core & Cardio chipper, specifically performed **with no jump**. |

#### Core and Metabolic Exercises

| Exercise | Specific Instruction/Variation from Source |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Mountain-climbers** | In the Metabolic Circuit, you count **each knee movement as 1** repetition (20 total). They should be performed **fast** in the Main Circuit. |
| **Leg-raise-to-hip-lift** | Performed for 12–15 reps during the even minutes of the EMOM. |
| **Shoulder-taps** | A finisher for the Upper + Core EMOM (100 total for time). Also performed in combination as **Plank Jack + Shoulder Tap**. |
| **Plank jacks** | Used for 60 reps in the Core & Cardio chipper. |
| **Fast-feet drop** | Involves squatting down, placing hands, and jumping back. |
| **High-knees** | Count the repetitions of the **right leg** (25 total). Also performed in place as part of the Dynamic Warm-Up. |

### Contextual Notes for Slide Creation

When building your slides, keep in mind the structure and progression rules provided in the sources:

1.  **Work/Rest Ratio:** Many sessions use specific work/rest periods, such as 30 seconds ON / 15 seconds OFF or 20 seconds ON / 10 seconds OFF (Tabata).
2.  **Rep Ranges:** If you finish a timed set (like push-ups) before 45 seconds during the EMOM, you must **hold a plank** for the remaining time.
3.  **Progression:** You are encouraged to swap to **harder variations** (e.g., single-leg burpees or clap push-ups) after four weeks instead of simply adding time.
4.  **Equipment:** All workouts are zero-equipment, but optional items like a **sturdy chair** (for decline push-ups/dips/step-ups) or a **backpack filled with books** (for adjustable weight) are listed if you wish to include equipment variations on your slides.
