## Research Domains (deep, multi-month)
### IVC / Indo-Aryan / Vedic — verified facts
- Nanda 343–321 BCE vs Cyrus 559–530 BCE = NO overlap (190yr gap). Real Cyrus contemporary = Bimbisara 544–493 BCE (14yr overlap).
- IVC (Rakhigarhi aDNA) = zero Steppe ancestry (Shinde 2019, Narasimhan 2019)
- No material proof of Vedic before ~1000 BCE; oldest physical Rigveda MS = 1040 CE (Nepal)
- Mitanni treaty KBo I 1/I 3 (~1380 BCE, Akkadian cuneiform, Bogazkoy Syria) = earliest indirect Vedic-name evidence, NOT in India
- Kikkuli Manual CTH 284 (~1345 BCE) = Indo-Aryan numerals more archaic than Rigveda
- Modern Hinduism (temple+bhakti) = formed 4th–15th CE, structurally diff from early Vedic
- Zoroaster name = only attested in Sasanian-era inscriptions

### Yoga/Ayurveda/Hindu Synthesis Model (mainstream view, research complete)
- L1 (pre-2000 BCE): IVC pre-Aryan — proto-Shaiva, ascetic, herbalism, goddess cults
- L2 (2000–1500 BCE): Steppe migration — Sanskrit, fire sacrifice, Indra pantheon
- L3 (1500–500 BCE): early Vedic gradual acculturation
- L4 (500 BCE): Shramana codified — Buddha/Mahavira NON-Vedic (Bronkhorst "Greater Magadha")
- L5 (500 BCE–800 CE): Brahmanical crisis, mass absorption
- Result: Modern Hinduism ≈ Vedic Sanskrit shell + ~70% pre-Aryan/Shramanic content
- Hypotheses (unproven, strong): IVC=Proto-Dravidian (Parpola); Shramana=pre-Aryan indigenous (Zimmer); Dasa+Harappan=Shaiva-Shakta Tantrism (Parpola)
