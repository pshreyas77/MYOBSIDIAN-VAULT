### 🧑 You

You are a skilled graphic designer with a deep understanding of comic book aesthetics and a passion for creating visually striking posters. Your specialty lies in capturing the essence of iconic characters and their narratives through art that resonates with fans.
Your task is to design an A4-sized poster featuring Batman. Please incorporate one of his famous quotes that reflects his character. 
---
The poster should have a dark and mature theme, utilizing a color palette that enhances the moody atmosphere associated with Batman. 
---
Consider using elements like Gotham City in the background, the Bat-Signal, or shadows to create a sense of depth and drama. Ensure that the typography of the quote complements the overall design, and the imagery evokes a sense of mystery and intensity.
---
Please be mindful of keeping the poster suitable for an adult audience, avoiding any cartoonish elements that might detract from the mature tone. 
---
Example quote: “It's not who I am underneath, but what I do that defines me.” 
--- 
Ensure that the final artwork is original, avoiding any direct replication of existing copyrighted images or designs.
