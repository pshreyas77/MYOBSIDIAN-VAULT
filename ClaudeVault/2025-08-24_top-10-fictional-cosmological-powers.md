## Top 10 Strongest Cosmologies in Fiction

**1. The Cthulhu Mythos (H.P. Lovecraft & Expanded)**
The ultimate cosmic horror universe featuring undimensional beings that exist completely outside of the concept of dimensions, including space, time, and more. Entities like Azathoth exist beyond all mathematical concepts of infinity and dimensionality.

**2. SCP Foundation Universe**
The SCP verse has entities like the Scarlet King who destroyed the Tree of Knowledge, which has a far bigger cosmology than Marvel Comics. Features infinite narrative hierarchies and conceptual entities that transcend traditional power scaling.

**3. Umineko no Naku Koro ni**
Contains infinite layers of witches existing in higher dimensions, with beings who view lower realities as complete fiction. The cosmology includes concepts of infinite meta-hierarchies where each layer sees the previous as stories.

**4. Twin Peaks (David Lynch Universe)**
Features the Lodge dimensions, non-linear time, and entities that exist outside conventional reality. The cosmology operates on dream logic and metaphysical principles that defy standard physics.

**5. DC Comics Cosmology**
Contains the Source, Presence, Overvoid, and multiple infinite dimensional realms. Includes concepts like the Bleed between universes and Monitor-level beings who view multiverses as stories.

**6. Marvel Comics Cosmology** 
Features entities like the One Above All, Living Tribunal, and infinite-dimensional beings. Contains multiple layers of infinity including the Far Shore and House of Ideas concepts.

**7. Tenchi Muyo Universe**
Contains hyperdimensional beings and infinite probability spaces. The Chousin exist beyond conventional dimensionality and can create infinite universes.

**8. World of Darkness**
Features the Umbra, infinite spirit realms, and entities that exist beyond physical reality. Contains concepts of infinite regression and metaphysical hierarchies.

**9. Masadaverse (Dies Irae/Kajiri Kamui Kagura)**
Contains concepts of Taikyoku values representing transcendental power levels, with gods who paint over reality itself with their desires.

**10. The Elder Scrolls Cosmology**
Features CHIM, Amaranth, and the Godhead - concepts where beings can achieve awareness of their fictional nature and transcend it. Contains infinite-dimensional Dream hierarchies.

These cosmologies are distinguished by their use of concepts that transcend traditional power scaling, often dealing with meta-fictional elements, infinite hierarchies, or beings that exist beyond mathematical concepts of infinity and dimensionality.
