---

### 🤖 Claude

This is a fascinating matchup! Let me break down each character's power levels and abilities:

**The Clear Winners:**
- **Superman** (especially Post-Crisis/Rebirth versions) has planet-moving strength, faster-than-light speed, near-invulnerability, and decades of combat experience against cosmic-level threats
- **Goku** (current manga) can shake universes during fights, has ultra instinct for perfect combat reflexes, and instant transmission for tactical advantages

**The Wild Card:**
- **Saitama** is intentionally written as a gag character who wins with "one punch" - his upper limits are undefined, making him either unstoppable or just very strong depending on interpretation

**The Mid-Tier:**
- **Metro Man** has Superman-like powers but less combat experience and fewer feats
- **Omni-Man** is incredibly powerful (planet-busting level) with centuries of combat experience
- **Invincible** (end of series) reaches similar levels to his father but with less experience

**The Clear Underdog:**
- **Homelander** is the weakest here - he struggles with military weapons and has no real combat training against equals

**My Take:** It likely comes down to **Superman vs. Goku**, the classic debate. Both have reality-warping levels of power, but Superman's versatility (heat vision, freeze breath, x-ray vision) might give him an edge over Goku's pure martial arts approach. However, Goku's fighting instincts and ability to quickly adapt and power up mid-fight could tip the scales.

**Saitama** could theoretically one-shot everyone if we take his gag nature literally, but that feels like cheating the premise.

What's your take? Are you leaning toward any particular character?
