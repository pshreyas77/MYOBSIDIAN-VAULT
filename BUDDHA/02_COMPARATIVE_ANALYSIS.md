## IVC → Buddhism/Jainism Connection (New Research)

### [[IVC_Shramana_Buddhism_Jainism_Comparison]]

**Comparative Table Summary:**

| Artifact | Buddhist Parallel | Jain Parallel | Connection |
|----------|-------------------|---------------|------------|
| Priest-King (~2000 BCE) | Buddha *dhyāna mudrā* | Tirthankara *kayotsarga* | Proto-ascetic template |
| Pashupati Seal | Compassion for beings | Ṛṣabhanātha bull emblem | Animal harmony/*ahimsa* |
| Bull seals | — | Bull = 1st Tirthankara symbol | Direct continuity |
| Swastika | *Aṣṭamaṅgala* | *Aṣṭamaṅgala* | Oldest continuous symbol |
| Pipal tree | Bodhi enlightenment | Tree *kevalajñāna* | Sacred tree worship |
| Great Bath | Water rituals | Ritual purity | Non-Vedic practice |

**Conclusion:** IVC culture → migrated east ~1900 BCE → survived in Gangetic substrate → reemerged as Śramaṇa tradition ~800 BCE → Buddhism/Jainism ~500 BCE
