# Periyar-Ambedkar TVK Model

TVK represents the closest living political experiment to the Periyar-Ambedkar framework in India today.

## Alignment with Type 1+2 Secularism
- Explicit anti-caste stance
- Secular constitution defense
- Anti-Hindutva (not anti-Hindu)
- Rationalist ideology (Periyar lineage)
- Youth + education focus
- No minority appeasement language - equality framing
- Vijay = OBC, speaks for OBC dignity (not vote bank)

## Caveats
- Never governed (zero administrative track record)
- Ideology ≠ governance ability
- Celebrity entry into politics - potential for ego-driven decisions
- TN already has DMK (Dravidian secular) - TVK eating DMK votes = BJP benefits via split

## Key Questions for Evaluation
1. Ticket distribution 2026: What % SC/ST/OBC candidates? What % upper caste?
2. Caste of key appointments
3. Will they ally with caste-based parties compromising anti-caste position?
4. Response to Hindu majoritarian pressure: Will Vijay soften anti-Hindutva stance?

## Ambedkarism vs. Electoral Reality
- Ambedkar's photo ≠ Ambedkar's politics
- Internal democracy: Does TVK practice what it preaches or become another family party?

## Personal Position
This framework is intellectually consistent and rare in Indian politics. Most "Ambedkarites" stop at reservation politics; most "Periyarists" stop at anti-Brahminism. TVK follows the complete rationalist framework both demanded.
