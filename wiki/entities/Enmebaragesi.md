# Enmebaragesi

## Overview

Enmebaragesi of Kish is the **oldest king CONFIRMED by physical artifact** — an inscribed vase fragment bearing his name.

## Evidence

| Type | Evidence | Date |
|------|----------|------|
| Physical artifact | Inscribed vase fragment | ~2900 BCE |
| Textual reference | Mentioned in Sumerian King List (SKL) | ~2100 BCE |

## Historical Context

- **Lived:** ~4,900 years ago
- **Dynasty:** First Dynasty of Kish
- **Sumerian King List:** First king in SKL who is also confirmed archaeologically
