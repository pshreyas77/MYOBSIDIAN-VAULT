# Siddhartha Gautama (Buddha)

**Type:** #entity
**Category:** Religious Figure, Philosopher
**Tradition:** Buddhism
**Dates:** c. 563-483 BCE
**Created:** 2024-04-17

## Summary
Founder of Buddhism. Born as prince in Lumbini (Nepal/India border). Attained enlightenment under Bodhi tree in Bodh Gaya. Taught for 45 years. Died in Kushinagar.

## Core Teachings
- **Four Noble Truths**
- **Eightfold Path**
- **Three Marks of Existence:** Anicca (impermanence), Dukkha (suffering), Anatta (non-self)
- **Middle Way** between asceticism and indulgence
- **Nirvana:** Liberation from suffering
