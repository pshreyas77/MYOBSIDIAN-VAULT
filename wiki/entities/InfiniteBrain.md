# InfiniteBrain

Personal knowledge management system with wiki structure, raw sources, and output organization.

## Overview

InfiniteBrain is a nested PKM within the vault featuring:
- `wiki/` — organized notes (concepts, summaries, articles)
- `raw/` — original source materials (articles, books, papers, podcasts)
- Structured knowledge domains: Technology, Science, Business, Personal Development

## Structure

```
InfiniteBrain/
├── wiki/
│   ├── index.md      # Knowledge domain index
│   ├── concepts/     # Concept notes
│   ├── summaries/     # Summary notes
│   └── articles/     # Article notes
└── raw/
    └── sources/      # Original source materials
```

## Knowledge Domains

1. **Technology** — Tech notes and concepts
2. **Science** — Scientific concepts and research
3. **Business** — Business strategy and insights
4. **Personal Development** — Self-improvement notes
