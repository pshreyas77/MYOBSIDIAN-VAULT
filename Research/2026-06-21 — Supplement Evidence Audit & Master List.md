## Contradiction Resolution — Berberine

**Doc 73 (credible):** Meta-analysis of 23 studies — 0.88kg loss over 3+ months. Mechanism: AMPK activation → hepatic glucose output ↓, intestinal absorption ↓.

**Doc 74 (overstated):** "Fat furnace", "thermogenesis", "caspase-3 brown fat activation" — all rodent data. No human RCTs support thermogenesis claim.

**Verdict:** Berberine is a **glycemic tool**, not a fat burner. Real for:
- Fasting glucose ↓ (ISSN 2024)
- HbA1c ↓ (post-intervention)
- Triglycerides ↓

Not proven for:
- Direct lipolysis in humans
- Thermogenesis (rodent only)
- Caspase-3 activation (rodent only)

**Recommendation for IR user:** 500mg with largest carb meal (dinner). Expect glucose disposal improvement, not visible fat loss.

## Extended 8-Supplement Deep Dive

**1. Creatine Monohydrate**
- **Evidence:** ISSN position stand 2017 (417 studies)
- **Mechanism:** Phosphocreatine ↑ → ATP regeneration → strength 5-10%
- **Water retention:** 0.3-0.8% body weight, intramuscular (leg/torso), not subcutaneous/face
- **Cognitive:** 2-4% working memory improvement (vegetarians: 8-12%)
- **Dose:** 3-5g/day (no loading needed)
- **India cost:** ₹600-900/month

**2. Berberine HCl**
- **Evidence:** Meta-analysis 23 RCTs (Doc 73)
- **Mechanism:** AMPK activation → ↓ hepatic glucose output, ↑ GLUT4 translocation
- **Human fat loss:** 0.88kg over 3+ months (statistically significant, clinically modest)
- **Glycemic:** Fasting glucose ↓ 0.5-0.8 mmol/L, HbA1c ↓ 0.5-1.0%
- **Lipids:** Triglycerides ↓ 0.3-0.5 mmol/L
- **Dose:** 500mg with largest carb meal (dinner for user)
- **India cost:** ₹600-900/month

**3. Spore-Based Probiotics**
- **Evidence:** LactoSpore (L. sporogenes) RCTs for T2D; Clostridium butyricum MBC (MIYAIRI 588)
- **Mechanism:** Gastric acid stable → colonize → SCFA (butyrate) production → gut barrier integrity
- **IR link:** Leaky gut → LPS endotoxemia → insulin resistance
- **Dose:** 2-4 billion CFU, AM empty stomach
- **India cost:** ₹800-1500/month
- **Curd-free:** No dairy, safe for user allergy

**4. Omega-3 (EPA+DHA)**
- **Evidence:** ISSN 2018 position stand
- **Inflammation:** IFOS-certified (oxidation <1.0 meq/kg) = anti-inflammatory; rancid = pro-inflammatory
- **Dose:** 2-4g combined EPA+DHA (not total "fish oil")
- **India cost:** ₹1200-2000/month (IFOS-certified: TrueBasics, NutraLife, iHerb imports)

**5. Magnesium Glycinate**
- **Evidence:** Meta-analysis 2017 (insulin sensitivity)
- **Mechanism:** Tyrosine kinase co-factor → insulin receptor signaling
- **Sleep:** Glycinate form → GABA modulation → sleep latency ↓
- **Dose:** 200-400mg elemental magnesium (PM)
- **India cost:** ₹250-400/month

**6. Vitamin D3**
- **Evidence:** 90% Indians deficient (sun avoidance, melanin)
- **Mechanism:** VDR receptor → insulin secretion, immune modulation
- **Dose:** 2000-4000 IU/day (with fat meal)
- **India cost:** ₹150-250/month

**7. Ashwagandha (KSM-66)**
- **Evidence:** RCTs for cortisol/stress (not fat loss)
- **Mechanism:** Cortisol ↓ 14-30% → stress-induced eating ↓
- **Dose:** 300-600mg PM
- **India cost:** ₹500-800/month
- **Verdict:** Adjunct for emotional eating, not direct lipolytic

**8. Psyllium Husk**
- **Evidence:** Fiber meta-analyses (satiety, glucose blunting)
- **Mechanism:** Gel formation → gastric emptying ↓ → glucose absorption ↓
- **Dose:** 5-10g pre-meal in 300ml water
- **India cost:** ₹200-300/month
- **Verdict:** Top-up if dietary fiber <25g/day
