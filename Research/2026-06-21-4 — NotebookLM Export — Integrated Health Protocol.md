## WORKOUT PROTOCOL — 3-Day Rotating PPL

### Why 45-60s Rest Instead of 2min?
- **2min rest:** For heavy loads (80-90% 1RM), CNS recovery
- **45-60s rest:** For light loads (40-60% 1RM), metabolic stress → hypertrophy via time-under-tension
- **4kg = ~40-60% 1RM** for most movements → short rest is critical

---

## PROGRESSION LOGIC (4kg Fixed Weight)

Since weight can't increase, progress via:

1. **Reps:** Add 1-2 reps per session until hitting top of range (20-25)
2. **Sets:** Add 1 set per exercise (max 5 sets)
3. **Tempo:** Slower eccentric (4-5 sec instead of 3)
4. **Rest reduction:** 60s → 50s → 45s → 40s
5. **Unilateral:** Single-leg/single-arm variations (e.g., Bulgarian split squats, 1-arm rows)
6. **Bodyweight upgrades:** Elevate feet for pike pushups, single-leg doorway rows

### 12-Week Progression Targets

| Metric | Week 1 | Week 4 | Week 8 | Week 12 |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|---------|
| Chest press reps/set | 15 | 18-20 | 20+ (add set) | 5 total sets |
| Doorway row reps | 15 | 20 | 20+ (slower tempo) | 4-sec eccentric |
| Goblet squat reps | 20 | 25 | 25+ (single-leg) | Bulgarian 15/leg |
| Rest between sets | 60s | 50s | 45s | 40s |
| Fasting glucose* | Baseline | ↓0.3-0.5 | ↓0.5-0.8 | ↓0.8-1.0 mmol/L |

*If monitoring with glucometer

**Deload Protocol:** Week 7 or 8 → reduce volume 50% (3 sets → 2 sets, or longer rest)
