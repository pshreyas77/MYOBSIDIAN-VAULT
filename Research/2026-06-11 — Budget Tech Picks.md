## For future Claude

Budget tech purchase recommendations for CSE student with HP ProBook i5-8350U (8GB RAM), Python/ML stack, Obsidian vault heavy, local AI focus. Profile: needs lightweight hardware, local-first setup, research + reading heavy workload.

> Note: All prices are approximate India-market ranges as of June 2026. Search on amazon.in / flipkart.com for current pricing. Do NOT trust static links — prices fluctuate.

---

## Priority Upgrades (Highest Impact)

### 1. RAM Upgrade — 8GB DDR4 SO-DIMM (16GB total)
**Cost:** ~₹1,500–2,000
**Why:** Single biggest bottleneck for local LLMs and ChromaDB
**Search:** "8GB DDR4 2400MHz SO-DIMM laptop"
**Note:** Verify ProBook max supported RAM (likely 32GB max, 2 slots)

### 2. USB-C Hub with HDMI + USB 3.0
**Cost:** ~₹800–1,200
**Why:** Dual-screen productivity for research/coding sessions
**Search:** "USB C hub HDMI USB3 Anker" or "Lapcare USB C hub"

### 3. 512GB SSD (if second slot available)
**Cost:** ~₹2,500–3,500
**Why:** Feren OS + Windows dual boot + model weights eat storage fast
**Search:** "Kingston A400 512GB SATA SSD"

### 4. Noise-cancelling earbuds (budget)
**Cost:** ~₹1,000–1,500
**Why:** Long research/reading sessions
**Search:** "Boats Airdopes 141" or "JBL Tune 215BT"

### 5. Kindle Paperwhite (refurbished/older gen)
**Cost:** ~₹4,000–5,000 (refurbished 10th gen)
**Why:** 100-book reading list; easier on eyes than laptop screen
**Search:** "Kindle Paperwhite refurbished amazon india"

## Secondary Upgrades (Good ROI)

### 6. Mechanical keyboard (budget)
**Cost:** ~₹1,500–2,000
**Why:** Coding + long writing sessions
**Search:** "Redragon K552 mechanical keyboard India"

### 7. USB microphone
**Cost:** ~₹2,000–2,500
**Why:** Voice input for AI projects; speech-to-text testing
**Search:** "MAONO AU-A04 USB microphone India"

### 8. Blue light blocking glasses
**Cost:** ~₹300–600
**Why:** Research + code all day; real eye strain impact
**Search:** "anti blue light glasses computer"

### 9. Portable 20,000mAh power bank
**Cost:** ~₹800–1,200
**Why:** Battery degraded on 8th gen (2017–2018 hardware)
**Search:** "Ambrane 20000mAh power bank" or "Syska 20K"

### 10. Thermal paste replacement
**Cost:** ~₹150–300
**Why:** i5-8350U probably throttling due to dried paste
**Search:** "thermal paste laptop CPU India"
**Note:** Immediate performance gain for CPU-heavy ML workloads

### 11. Xiaomi Smart Band 8 / Mi Band
**Cost:** ~₹2,000–2,500
**Why:** Sleep tracking, reminders for long work sessions
**Search:** "Xiaomi Smart Band 8 India"

### 12. 1TB external HDD
**Cost:** ~₹3,500
**Why:** Model weights, dataset backups, Obsidian vault backups
**Search:** "Seagate 1TB external hard drive India"
