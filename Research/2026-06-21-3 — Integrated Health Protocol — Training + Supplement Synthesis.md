## Integrated Protocol — 12 Weeks

### Weekly Schedule (Rotating PPL + Supplement Timing)

| Day | Training | Supplement Focus | Meal Timing |
|-----|----------|------------------|-------------|

**Rotation logic:** Train every other day, rest after Legs or when fatigue accumulates. Not fixed Mon-Sun — cycle based on recovery.

## Supplement + Training Interactions

### Pre-Workout (30-60 min before)

| Goal | Supplement | Rationale |
|------|------------|-----------|
| **Optional** | Psyllium 5g in 300ml water | Only if fiber <25g/day; slows gastric emptying → sustained energy during workout |
| **Skip** | Berberine pre-workout | No evidence for performance; save for post-meal glucose disposal |

**Note:** No caffeine-based pre-workout needed — 4kg protocol relies on metabolic stress, not CNS stimulation.

---

### Post-Workout (within 2 hrs, not critical "anabolic window")

| Supplement | Dose | Mechanism |
|------------|------|-----------|
| **Whey concentrate** | 20-30g | Leucine → mTOR activation; safe ≤30g/day for IR (J Nutr 2023) |
| **Creatine monohydrate** | 5g | Phosphocreatine replenishment; intramuscular water (leg/torso, not face) |

**Timing synergy:** Magnesium + berberine NOT post-workout — reserve for PM/dinner to avoid overlapping insulin signaling with post-workout glucose disposal (already elevated from training).

---

### PM / Dinner (largest carb meal)

| Supplement | Dose | Interaction with Training |
|------------|------|---------------------------|
| **Berberine HCl** | 500mg | AMPK activation → hepatic glucose output ↓; training-day advantage: GLUT4 translocation already elevated from workout |
| **Magnesium glycinate** | 400mg | Insulin receptor tyrosine kinase co-factor; sleep quality ↑ → recovery ↑ |
| **Synergy** | — | Berberine + magnesium = dual-pathway insulin sensitization (AMPK + receptor signaling) |

**Why dinner?** User's largest carb meal → maximal glucose disposal opportunity. Training earlier in day primes glucose uptake; berberine amplifies.

### AM (empty stomach, rest days too)

| Supplement | Dose | Rationale |

**Take with:** Small fat source (handful nuts, ghee in coffee) for D3 absorption.

## Contraindications / Skip List

### Redundant Supplements (don't stack)

| Skip | Why |
|------|-----|
| **Alpha-lipoic acid** | Same AMPK pathway as berberine; redundant |
| **Myo-inositol** | PCOS/fertility focus; not relevant for 22M |
| **BCAAs** | Whey is complete EAA profile; mTOR overactivation risk |
| **CLA / Garcinia / Raspberry ketones** | Rodent-only thermogenesis; zero human fat loss evidence |

### Training Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Correction |
|---------|------------|
| Taking 2-min rest (traditional strength protocol) | 45-60s ONLY — metabolic stress requires accumulated fatigue |
| Skipping Pull day | 5-PDF set had 0 pull days — structural imbalance risk (shoulder impingement) |
| Progressing by adding weight | Can't — 4kg fixed. Progress via reps → sets → tempo → rest reduction → unilateral |
| Post-workout berberine | Overlaps with natural post-workout glucose disposal; reserve for dinner |
