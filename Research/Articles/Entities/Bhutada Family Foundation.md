# Bhutada Family Foundation

**Type:** 501(c)(3) Family Foundation  
**Location:** Houston, Texas, USA  
**Status:** active  
**Confidence:** high (tax filing data)

## Overview

The Bhutada family is cited as a major financial pillar of the RSS ecosystem. The foundation makes grants to Hindu organizations in the U.S. and India, with a documented track record of funding Sangh Parivar affiliates.
