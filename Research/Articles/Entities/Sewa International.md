# Sewa International

**Type:** Sangh Parivar Affiliate / Funding Hub  
**Founded:** 1998 (USA)  
**Status:** active  
**Confidence:** high

## Overview

Sewa International is the U.S.-based arm of the RSS-affiliated Sewa Bharat, operating as a charity and the primary financial routing mechanism for Sangh Parivar funding from the United States to India.

## Documented Funding

| Funder | Amount | Year |
|--------|--------|------|
| Bhutada Family Foundation | ~$496,495 | 2005–2018 |
| Jack Dorsey (former Twitter CEO) | $2.5 million | 2021 |

### COVID-19 Relief Funds
Sewa International received significant U.S. federal COVID-19 relief funds, which were subsequently deployed for Sangh Parivar activities in India.

## Role

- Acts as a 501(c)(3) pass-through for diaspora donations to RSS-affiliated projects in India
- Described in investigative reports as routing funds to Sangh operations
