- [[Research/Deep/2026-07-01 — Name Shreyas Linguistic Etymology Across Language Families]] — Ingested linguistic etymology of name Shreyas (श्रेयस्) across 5 language families: Indo-Aryan (Vedic Sanskrit, Avestan, Pali, 10 modern IA languages), Dravidian (Tamil/Telugu/Malayalam/Kannada as Sanskrit borrowings), and Mandarin (transliterations 史瑞亚斯/施热亚). Key finding: śreyas = comparative form of śrī "prosperity/beauty/auspiciousness"; core meaning "better/superior/excellent" remains semantically stable across all families. Famous Vedic usage: Katha Upanishad śreyas/preyas distinction (higher good vs. pleasant).
